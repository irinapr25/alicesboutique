About us

A few words about our cattery

Alice’s Boutique is a certified cattery, officially registered in the WCF (the World Cat Federation) system and has been a member of the MoonCat Club since 2020.

The cattery hold a certificate of registration for veterinary supervision.

We specialise in breeding Maine  Coon, Abyssinian, and Canadian Sphynx kittens.

Our cattery is focused on getting healthy, beautiful kittens that fully meet the established standard of their breed.

Our home cattery is located in Latvia. We live outside the city, in an environmentally clean area.

Our pets grow up in an atmosphere of love, attention, and care, making them the perfect companions for your family.

Love and Care

Every kitten in our cattery is raised in an atmosphere of love and care, making them ideal companions for your family.

From the first day of their lives, every kitten learns to interact with people, as all kittens grow up with us.

The kittens are ready to move to a new home at the earliest at the age of 3.5 months, which guarantees their maturity and ability to adapt.

Health and Safety

All our kittens receive the necessary vaccinations according to their age and undergo deworming according to the schedule.

They receive proper medical attention to ensure their health.

Each kitten is microchipped.

They are already accustomed to using a litter box and scratching post, which significantly facilitates their adaptation to a new home.

Consultations and Recommendations

We provide consultations on choosing food, creating a menu to ensure proper nutrition and variety.

We offer recommendations on selecting necessary accessories for taking care of your new friend.

We provide guidance on the kitten’s show career.

Assistance is provided when relocating kittens to other cities or countries.

Documentation and Contracts

Almost any of our kittens can be sold as a pet-class (pet) or as breed-class (with breeding rights) and show-class (for shows).

Sale of kittens for breeding purposes is restricted and allowed only to officially registered catteries!

For each of our kittens, we provide a pedigree, an International Veterinary Passport, and a Contract with the buyer.

We also provide assistance in obtaining all necessary export documents for transportation to other countries.

Breed Standard

We adhere to high breed standards to ensure that our kittens embody all the distinctive traits of their breeds.

Only physically and mentally healthy cats, tested for genetic diseases, are allowed into our breeding program.

We actively participate in cat shows and hold exhibition titles.

We are constantly learning, attending courses, seminars, studying pedigrees, and breed lines.

Diversity and Transparency

In our cattery, we offer a variety of breeds, allowing you to choose the one that aligns with your preferences.

We value openness and transparency in our relationships with clients, providing all necessary information about our kittens.

We are available for communication with the owners of our kittens 24/7. You can always share your joy with us or tell us about a problem.

Maine Coon

Greatness in the Cat World

The Maine Coon is a treasure of the feline world, embodying both grandeur and tenderness. These colossal cats have an imposing appearance, but beneath their muscular build lies a gentle nature.

The Maine Coon’s expressive eyes and unique coat patterns, ranging from striped to solid, give them individuality. Their long, fluffy tails and distinctive ear tufts make them truly unique.

Maine Coons have a refined temperament and are incredibly calm and tolerant. They are friendly, sociable and get on well with other pets and children.

Genuine Love in Every Fur
Abyssinian

Elegance and Grace

The Abyssinian is the epitome of refined elegance and natural grace. Its slender, graceful body seems made for movement.

The coat of the Abyssinian is surprisingly soft and silky, like velvet. The paws are graceful and light, while the tail is long and curved, always in motion.

These cats have incredible agility and playfulness. Their intelligence and curiosity allow them to quickly learn new skills, develop their minds, and perform various commands and tricks.

Abyssinians make amazing companions, as they form strong bonds with their owners and actually enjoy spending time with people. They are eager to show you attention and loyalty in return.

Love at first sight
Canadian Sphynx

Smooth Charm

The Canadian Sphynx has a unique appearance, characterised by an almost complete absence of fur and distinctive wrinkles. Its slender and lithe body, long limbs and tail, large ears and expressive eyes create an unforgettable image.

Their skin is warm and soft to the touch, covered with a fine coat of down that ranges from classic pink to more exotic shades.

Regular showers and wet wipes are an integral part of caring for this remarkable creature.

These warmth-loving creatures are renowned for their friendliness and sociability and enjoy the company of their owners.

The magic of their enchanting gaze, the smooth and harmonious lines of their body, their unusual appearance, their delightfully affectionate nature and their high level of intelligence — you won’t be able to resist their charm.
